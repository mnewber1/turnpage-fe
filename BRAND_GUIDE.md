# Turnpage Brand Guide

## Overview

**Turnpage** is a social reading app that connects book lovers through shared stories. It's a book club platform where readers discover clubs, discuss books, and build meaningful connections around their favorite reads.

### Tagline
**"Where Stories Bring Strangers Together"**

### Mission
Connect readers who share a passion for books. Enable meaningful discussions, help users discover their next great read, and foster community through the shared love of storytelling.

### Value Proposition
- Join and create book clubs around specific books
- Real-time chat discussions with spoiler protection
- Discover trending clubs and connect with like-minded readers
- Track reading progress and share with your club
- @mention other readers and react to messages

---

## Target Audience

### Primary
- **Age:** 18-45
- **Interests:** Avid readers, book club enthusiasts, people seeking community
- **Behavior:** Read 10+ books per year, active on social platforms, enjoy discussing books
- **Pain points:** Difficulty finding local book clubs, wanting to discuss books without spoilers, seeking reading recommendations from real people

### Secondary
- Casual readers looking to read more
- People new to a city seeking social connections
- Book influencers and content creators

## Key Features

### 1. Book Clubs
- Create or join clubs centered around specific books
- Public, private, and community club types
- Real-time group chat with all members

### 2. Spoiler Protection
- Mark messages with spoiler levels (Mild, Major, Ending, Guess)
- Chapter-based spoiler tags
- Auto-reveal based on user's reading progress

### 3. Social Features
- @mention other readers
- React to messages with themed reactions
- View member profiles and reading activity

### 4. Discovery
- Trending clubs based on activity
- Genre-based browsing
- Community clubs for genre discussions

### 5. Reading Progress
- Track current chapter
- Share progress with club members
- Finish book celebrations

## Competitive Positioning

### What Makes Turnpage Different
| Turnpage | Goodreads | Generic Book Apps |
|----------|-----------|-------------------|
| Real-time chat discussions | Reviews & ratings | Static content |
| Intimate club communities | Mass social network | Solo experience |
| Smart spoiler protection | No spoiler controls | No discussion features |
| Mobile-first experience | Desktop-focused | Varies |

### Key Differentiators
1. **Chat-first:** Real conversations, not reviews
2. **Club-focused:** Small communities, not mass followers
3. **Spoiler-smart:** Read at your own pace safely
4. **Mobile-native:** Designed for iOS from the ground up
