Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a book club?

Search for the book you're currently reading and tap "Find Readers" to see clubs discussing that book. You can join an existing club or start your own.

How do spoiler tags work?

When sending a message, tap the eye icon to mark it as a spoiler. You can specify the chapter number, and the message will be hidden from readers who haven't reached that point yet.

Can I be in multiple book clubs?

Yes! Free users can join up to 5 active clubs. Premium members have unlimited club access.

How do I delete my account?

Go to your Profile, scroll to the bottom, and tap "Delete Account." This will permanently remove your account and all associated data.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Tap and hold on any message to see the report option. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours.
