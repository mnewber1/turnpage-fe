Overview

Turnpage is a social reading app that connects book lovers through shared stories. It's a book club platform where readers discover clubs, discuss books, and build meaningful connections around their favorite reads.

"Where Stories Bring Strangers Together"

Mission

Connect readers who share a passion for books. Enable meaningful discussions, help users discover their next great read, and foster community through the shared love of storytelling.

Value Proposition

  • Join and create book clubs around specific books
  • Real-time chat discussions with spoiler protection
  • Discover trending clubs and connect with like-minded readers
  • Track reading progress and share with your club
  • @mention other readers and react to messages

Target Audience

Primary Audience

  • Age: 18-45
  • Interests: Avid readers, book club enthusiasts, people seeking community
  • Behavior: Read 10+ books per year, active on social platforms, enjoy discussing books

Pain Points

  • Difficulty finding local book clubs
  • Wanting to discuss books without spoilers
  • Seeking reading recommendations from real people

Secondary Audience

  • Casual readers looking to read more
  • People new to a city seeking social connections
  • Book influencers and content creators

Brand Personality

Voice & Tone

  • Warm & Welcoming - Like a cozy bookshop or a friend's living room
  • Thoughtful - Respectful of readers' time and preferences
  • Community-focused - Emphasizes connection over competition
  • Literate but accessible - Smart without being pretentious

Design Principles

  • Warm & Cozy: Cream backgrounds evoke the feeling of book pages
  • Organic: Rounded corners (12-20px), soft shadows
  • Readable: High contrast text, generous spacing
  • Elegant Simplicity: Clean layouts that let content breathe

Key Features

Book Clubs

Create or join clubs centered around specific books. Public, private, and community club types with real-time group chat.

Spoiler Protection

Mark messages with spoiler levels (Mild, Major, Ending, Guess). Chapter-based tags with auto-reveal based on reading progress.

Social Features

@mention other readers, react to messages with themed reactions, view member profiles and reading activity.

Discovery

Trending clubs based on activity, genre-based browsing, and community clubs for genre discussions.

Reading Progress

Track current chapter, share progress with club members, and celebrate finishing books.
