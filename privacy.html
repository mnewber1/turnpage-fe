Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Turnpage ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Account information (name, email address)
  • Profile information (bio, reading preferences, favorite genres)
  • Content you create (messages, book club participation)
  • Reading activity (books you're reading, progress)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Connect you with other readers and book clubs
  • Send you notifications about activity in your clubs
  • Respond to your comments and questions

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information:

  • With other users as part of the app's social features (your profile, messages in clubs)
  • With service providers who assist in operating our app
  • If required by law or to protect rights and safety

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Choices

You can update or delete your account information at any time through the app. You can also contact us to request deletion of your data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@turnpage.app.
